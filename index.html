· where you were traveling to · who was traveling with you

3 years ago, I had a trip to Orchid Island in Nha Trang city with my friends. It was a refreshing experience when I first came to an island. We included 7 people so we booked a taxi to move to the harbor. On the way to the harbor it was quite smooth, almost nothing happened. This was probably my worst trip, all my plans were almost ruined.
